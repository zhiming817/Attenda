# Attenda DApp Quick Demo Guide

## â±ï¸ 3-Minute Demo (English)

### 1. Connect Wallet
- Open the Attenda web app.
- Click the wallet button in the top right and connect your Sui wallet.

### 2. Create an Event
- Click `Create Event` in the navigation bar.
- Fill in event details (name, capacity, etc.) and submit.
- Wait for the transaction to complete. You will see your event in `My Events`.

### 3. Purchase a Ticket
- Go to `Browse Events` and select an event.
- Click `Get Ticket (Free)`.
- Approve the transaction in your wallet.
- After success, check your ticket in `My Tickets`.

### 4. View & Decrypt Ticket
- Click a ticket in `My Tickets`.
- Click `Decrypt & View Ticket`.
- Approve the signature request in your wallet.
- The encrypted ticket details (location, QR code, etc.) will be revealed.

### 5. Check-In via QR Code Scan (Organizer Only)
- Go back to the event detail page as the event organizer.
- Click `ğŸ« Check-In Attendees` button in the organizer actions section.
- Click `ğŸ« Start Scanning` to activate the camera.
- Have the attendee show their decrypted ticket QR code.
- Scan the QR code - the system will automatically verify and check in the attendee.
- Success! The attendee receives an Attendance NFT, and the ticket is marked as used.

### 6. Done!
- You have experienced the full on-chain event flow: creation, ticketing, decryption, and check-in verification.
